# Attorney Assistant Brand Guide

This Storybook documents the complete brand system for Attorney Assistant's marketing website.

## Brand Identity

Attorney Assistant solves the operational problems that prevent law firms from growing — intake failure, follow-up gaps, admin chaos, and scaling bottlenecks. We handle the work firms need but hate to manage.

## Core Positioning

- We are an **operations problem-solver**, not a staffing vendor
- We **co-manage** operations alongside firm teams — we don't outsource from a distance
- The three problems we solve: **intake failure**, **admin chaos**, **growth stagnation**
- Firms think they need more leads — they actually need to stop leaking the ones they have

## Contents

- **Colors** — Brand color palette with hex values, usage guidelines, and accessibility contrast ratios
- **Typography** — Font families (Sora + Inter), weights, and heading/body hierarchy
- **Buttons** — Primary, secondary, and variant button styles
- **Cards** — Blog cards, testimonial cards, and feature cards
- **Hero** — Hero banner variations with floating orb animations
- **CTA Banner** — Call-to-action section variants
- **Logo** — Logo usage and placement guidelines
- **Icons** — Icon set used across the site
- **Patterns** — Reusable design patterns including floating orbs, scroll reveal, grain overlays, DayTimeline, Scrollytelling, 404 page, and landing page patterns

## Ambient Animations

Hero sections use floating gradient orbs for subtle depth:
- **Blue orb**: `radial-gradient(circle, rgba(80,167,221,0.15–0.22) ...)` — multi-stop keyframe animation
- **Orange orb**: `radial-gradient(circle, rgba(249,166,48,0.12–0.22) ...)` — multi-stop keyframe animation
- **Placement**: Homepage hero has 3 orbs (custom inline). All subpage heroes (`Hero.astro`) have 2 orbs. 404 page has 2 orbs. Landing pages have their own orb instances.
- All animations respect `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`

## Component Placement

| Component | Page | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| `DayTimeline` | Homepage (`/`) | Moved from services page. First item starts expanded. Accordion expand/collapse. 8 time slots from 7:30 AM–11:45 PM. |
| `Scrollytelling` | Services page (`/services`) | Moved from homepage. Lightened navy background (`#132d46`), blue-tinted particles, toggle between without/with states. |
| `Hero.astro` | All subpage heroes | 2 floating orbs, white background with mesh gradient. |

## Special Pages

- **404 page** — Dark navy (`bg-brand-950`) with grain overlay, 2 floating orbs (`rgba(80,167,221,0.22)` + `rgba(249,166,48,0.22)`), multi-stop keyframe animations, gradient "404" text (white → gold → blue), two CTAs.
- **Landing page** (`/landing/lp-marketing-waste`) — Dark hero with inline form, flow diagram, stat cards, problem list, numbered fix grid, side-by-side comparison columns, numbered steps with connectors, dark proof cards section.

## Brand Positioning

We are an **operations problem-solver**, not a staffing vendor. Avoid language like "dedicated assistants" or "staffing company." We co-manage operations alongside firm teams — we don't outsource from a distance.
